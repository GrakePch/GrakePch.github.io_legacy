9-SQuares Texture is a Minecraft resourcepack made by GrakePch. Its predecessors, 3x3 Texture and Nine-Squares Texture, which were based on the famous resourcepack, oCd Pack, were stopped updating and offering. The brand new 9-SQuares which uses a more compatible classic 16×16 resolution to create 3×3 style is completely remade.
